MOBILE SALES AND PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS

In this project i analyzed mobile phone sales data to uncover performance trends, product demand patterns, and customer purchasing behaviors. Using an interactive dashboard, it visualized monthly sales fluctuations, identified top-performing models and brands, and highlighted the most profitable regions. The analysis also explored customer demographics, such as age group, gender, and purchase preferences, as well as the impact of storage size and pricing on revenue. These insights provided a clear picture of market dynamics, helping to pinpoint growth opportunities, guide inventory planning, and support targeted marketing strategies.

Customer Satisfaction & Loyalty Analytics

I Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to evaluate customer satisfaction and loyalty across 10 countries, uncovering key factors driving repeat purchases and retention. The analysis revealed that product quality and packaging scored highest in satisfaction, while price perception, support availability, and brand reputation lagged behind. The Regional mapping showed certain locations with higher non-repeat buyer rates, indicating weaker loyalty. And Gender-based insights highlighted that female customers, though valuing features and customer service, reported lower satisfaction and loyalty than males, who favored ease of use, quality, and packaging. While Age analysis showed the 55–60 group as the most loyal, while younger segments were less engaged.
